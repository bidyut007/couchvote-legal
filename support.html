What is CouchVote?

CouchVote helps you and your group decide what to watch. Pick a mood, choose your streaming services, and swipe through movies and TV shows that are actually available to you. Create a group, vote together in real time, and match on something everyone wants to watch.

Frequently asked questions

How do I sign in?

Tap Sign in with Apple or Continue with Google on the login screen. CouchVote only uses your name, email and profile picture to create your account — nothing is posted anywhere on your behalf.

How do groups work?

Create a group from the Groups tab and share the invite code (or link). When everyone has joined, you all swipe the same titles. The moment every member wants the same title, it's a match. CouchVote even matches you instantly on titles you'd each already liked on your own.

Why don't I see a title I expected?

Suggestions are limited to titles available on the streaming services and country you selected. Update these any time in Profile → Preferences, or adjust the filters on the Discover screen (mood, type, language, runtime).

The "where to watch" info looks wrong.

Streaming availability is provided by JustWatch via TMDB and can change as titles move between services. We refresh it regularly; if something still looks off, email us with the title name and your country.

How do I save things for later?

Use My List to keep titles you want to watch and mark the ones you've already seen. You can search your list or add a title directly.

How do I send feedback or report a bug?

In the app: Profile → Send feedback. You can attach a screenshot. Every message reaches us directly.

Manage your account

Delete your account and data

In the app: Profile → Delete account. This permanently deletes your account, profile, activity, group memberships and any groups you created — immediately and irreversibly.
